------------------------> 1. SED <------------------------

What Sed does?

	SED works on line numbers of a file

		-> It can help you print a line number content

		-> We can do find and replace operations on the content of file

		-> We can delete content of a line 

		-> It helps us perform insertion,append before and after a particular line number
